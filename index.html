What is Mapsense?

A High-Speed Mapping Engine

  • Ingest and index up to TB of data

  • Lightning-fast search & filtering

  • Smart Density-Sampling for amazing legibility and performance

  • On-Prem and in-cloud support

Developer Tools

Mapsense.js

  • Free access to our open-source Mapsense.js library. 

  • Unlimited basemap styles and possibilities. 

  • Based on polymaps.js and D3.js

Mapsense Data

  • Query, extract, and mashup rich public data, such as OpenStreetMap and US Demographics

  • Publish open data to the world
